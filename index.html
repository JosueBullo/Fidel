Our Academic Strands

Explore the different academic strands offered at Fidel Zarceno National High School.

Technical-Vocational-Livelihood (TVL)

At Fidel Zarceno National High School (FDNHS), the TVL (Technical-Vocational-Livelihood) track offers specialized programs in various fields, including ICT (Information and Communications Technology), Industrial Arts, and Home Economics. These programs are designed to equip students with practical skills and knowledge that are essential for future careers and entrepreneurship. The TVL track focuses on hands-on learning, enabling students to become proficient in technical areas and ensuring they are prepared for both employment and further education after graduation.

Humanities and Social Sciences (HUMSS)

At Fidel Zarceno National High School (FDNHS), the HUMSS (Humanities and Social Sciences) track is designed for students interested in fields such as psychology, sociology, education, law, political science, and communication. This track helps students develop critical thinking, communication, and analytical skills, preparing them for careers in social work, teaching, journalism, law, and other related professions. HUMSS focuses on understanding human behavior, social dynamics, and cultural studies, providing students with the knowledge to contribute meaningfully to society in various professional capacities.

Science, Technology, Engineering, and Mathematics (STEM)

At Fidel Zarceno National High School (FDNHS), the STEM (Science, Technology, Engineering, and Mathematics) track is designed for students with a strong interest in fields such as engineering, information technology, medicine, and scientific research. This track emphasizes critical problem-solving skills, innovation, and practical application of scientific principles.

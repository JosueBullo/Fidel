2024 National Tech-Voc Day

August 29, 2024 To commemorate the 2024 National Tech-Voc Day, the Fidel Zarceno National High School celebrates it with a Forum and Skills Competition in ICT, Home Economics, and Industrial Arts. Dr. Ariel Reckonette Villanueva, Vocational School Superintendent of Leon Ganzon Polytechnic College (TESDA Administered School) serves as our guest speaker. It was a fun-filled day. Thank you, everyone.

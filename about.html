Fidel Zarceno National High School

Fidel Zarceno National High School (FDNHS) is a prestigious educational institution committed to providing high-quality education to students. Located in the heart of the community, FDNHS focuses on holistic development, academic excellence, and the fostering of critical thinking and leadership skills. The school offers a wide range of academic tracks, including TVL (Technical-Vocational-Livelihood), HUMSS (Humanities and Social Sciences), STEM (Science, Technology, Engineering, and Mathematics), and others, to cater to the diverse interests and career aspirations of its students.

At FDNHS, the goal is to nurture well-rounded individuals who are prepared not only for higher education but also for success in their future careers and as responsible citizens of society. The school emphasizes values such as integrity, excellence, and social responsibility, ensuring that students graduate with a strong foundation in both academics and character. Through various programs and activities, FDNHS strives to create a supportive and dynamic learning environment that empowers students to reach their full potential.

Our Mission

At Fidel Zarceno National High School, our mission is to offer a comprehensive and inclusive educational experience that not only fosters academic excellence but also nurtures the personal growth and emotional well-being of our students. We are committed to cultivating critical thinking skills, a sense of social responsibility, and leadership qualities that will empower students to contribute meaningfully to their communities. By offering diverse opportunities and resources, we prepare students to adapt to an ever-changing global landscape and become proactive citizens in society.

Our Vision

Our vision at FZNHS is to become a leading educational institution that sets the standard for academic excellence, innovation, and community service. We aspire to empower students with the skills, knowledge, and confidence to succeed not only in their academic pursuits but also in their personal and professional lives. Through a well-rounded curriculum that emphasizes holistic development, FZNHS envisions shaping a new generation of individuals who are not only intellectually capable but also compassionate and globally competitive, contributing to the betterment of society in a meaningful and lasting way.

Core Values

  • Excellence: We are committed to achieving the highest standards of academic and personal growth, continuously striving to improve and reach new heights.
  • Integrity: We uphold the principles of honesty, accountability, and transparency in every action, building a culture of trust and respect.
  • Innovation: We encourage creativity and forward-thinking approaches, fostering an environment where students can explore new ideas and embrace change with confidence.

Our Community

We are proud of the vibrant and diverse community we have created at FZNHS. Our students, teachers, and staff come from various backgrounds, and together we celebrate the uniqueness of each individual while working towards a common goal: creating a better future.
